About us


Thanks for visiting the enchanted land of Turn n' Cough Candy online candy store! Here at Turn n' Cough, we pride ourselves on our vast selection and excellent customer service. Since 1998, we've strictly upheld a tradition of candy excellence! Did you have a favorite candy when you were a kid? Do you have kids of your own who like unusual or hard-to-find confections? Chances are, you can find everything you need right here. From nostalgic to new, minty to sour, tiny to giant-sized, liquid to chunky, lavender to silver, hard to chewy, gourmet to gummy boogers, we've got it all!
